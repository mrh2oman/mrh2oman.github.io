    • Hello, and welcome to my little space on the web. My name is David and I am the creator and owner of this website.

    I was born in Kansas City, Missouri as a founding member of the most powerful generation (according to recent media reports) ever -- the Millennials. My time in Missouri was short and before the age of three I lived in Kansas, Oklahoma, and Louisiana. From the ages of three to 16 I lived in America's favorite Bugs Bunny gif - Florida. In that span I lived in Deerfield Beach and Delray Beach attending just two schools - Just For Kids and Zion Lutheran Christan School.

    While living in Florida I would return to Kansas to visit family and attend the University of Kansas basketball camp with Roy Williams . From an early age, my Aunt indoctrinated me to the ways of the Phog and it didn't take much to get me hooked. I loved KU basketball and even meet Wilt Chamberlin in Florida at his restaurant, the now closed...wait for it...Wilt Chamberlin's. While KU was losing to Duke, Mr. Chamberlin sat at the bar watching the game and I was busy in his arcade section winning enough tickets so I could get a photo of him to sign.

    I was raised to be a Jayhawk, so I figured that's where I would go to college. Since I lived in Florida, I didn't think there was much of a chance given the cost of out-of-state tuition. Luckily (or unluckily depending on your point of view) my parents job had become less than enjoyable and I was given two options. Either we could stay in Florida until I graduated or we could move to Kansas. I choose to move to Kansas as I was interested in a change of scenery (I attended the same school from 5th grade to 10th grade) and I wanted to go to the University of Kansas.
